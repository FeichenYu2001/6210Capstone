General Interview Questions

  1. Tell me about yourself.
  2. Why do you want to work here?
  3. What are your greatest strengths?
  4. What are your weaknesses?
  5. Why did you leave your last job?
  6. Describe a challenging situation and how you handled it.
  7. Where do you see yourself in five years?
  8. What motivates you?
  9. Tell me about a time you made a mistake.
  10. How do you handle stress or pressure?
  11. What are your salary expectations?
  12. Why should we hire you?
  13. What are your career goals?
  14. What do you know about our company?
  15. Tell me about a successful project you've worked on.
  16. Describe your leadership style.
  17. How do you prioritize tasks?
  18. Have you ever disagreed with a manager? How did you handle it?
  19. What are you passionate about?
  20. How do you handle failure?
  21. Describe a time you worked as part of a team.
  22. How do you stay organized?
  23. Tell me about a time you went above and beyond.
  24. What are your hobbies or interests outside of work?
  25. How do you adapt to change?
  26. What is your greatest professional achievement?
  27. Describe a time you had to learn something quickly.
  28. What do you value most in a workplace?
  29. How do you handle tight deadlines?
  30. Have you ever dealt with a difficult coworker?
  31. What’s your approach to problem-solving?
  32. Describe a time you failed to meet a goal.
  33. How do you balance multiple projects?
  34. What tools or techniques do you use for time management?
  35. What type of work environment do you prefer?
  36. What makes you unique?
  37. How do you handle feedback?
  38. What would your previous manager say about you?
  39. What professional development have you pursued recently?
  40. What do you consider your biggest accomplishment?
  41. Are you a self-starter?
  42. How do you define success?
  43. What’s your availability?
  44. What do you expect from a manager?
  45. Have you led a team before?
  46. Do you prefer working independently or with a team?
  47. Tell me about a time you had to persuade someone.
  48. What would you do if you were running behind schedule?
  49. What do you do when you don't know how to complete a task?
  50. What is your greatest learning experience?
